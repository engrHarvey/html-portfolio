The Best Movies According to Harvey

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Cold Eyes

Ha Yoon-joo joins Hwang's police division. As soon as she joins the team, robbers rob the bank. However, because of the masks and skills, they cannot find any shreds of evidence. Meanwhile, James, the leader of the criminals, plans something else.

Your Name.

Two teenagers share a profound, magical connection upon discovering they are swapping bodies. Things manage to become even more complicated when the boy and girl decide to meet in person.

Pandora

A nuclear power plant is built in a rural town, but soon an earthquake causes the power plant to explode, exposing the people to radiation.
